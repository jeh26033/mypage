This is me

My name is Joseph Harskamp, though my friends just call me Joe. I grew up on a farm in Northwest Iowa, but currently live in Northern Virginia with my wife and dog.

I learned a lot living on a farm, like the value of a solid work ethic, honesty, and respect. Those values stayed with me, and are part and parcel of who I am. I enjoy tinkering with things, whether it's an old car, a website, or some old gadget I've found, my curiosity is what fuels my passion. I love to take things apart, examine their inner workings, and reassemble them to a state better than before.

I love learning and growing in every aspect of my life. I enjoy hiking, video games, craft beer, board games, and of course, programming. I'm a bit quirky, with an artistic streak, but little skill in physical mediums like paints or pencils, so I use pixels to express myself. I like to think that I look at things from different angles than most people. I also have great people skills, and like to be around others, though I'm just as fine all by myself.

If you want a resume, there's a link on this page, or you can contact me for any information you may need.
